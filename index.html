About

Builder, founder,
self-appointed coffee SME.

Suraj Pabba operates at the intersection of AI, data, and the stubborn belief that software should actually work for the people using it.

Over a decade across KPMG, Salesforce, iManage, and CNA Insurance has produced one recurring observation: the gap between what data systems can do and what end users experience is almost always a design problem, not a technology problem.

He's shipped production AI at startups, sold enterprise SaaS to Fortune 500s, and co-founded InField AI out of the Antler accelerator. These days he consults on data management strategy at CNA while quietly building his next chapter in home services.
